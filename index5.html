My favourite Website is : Wikipedia

Wikipedia is a free, online, multilingual encyclopedia written and maintained by a global community of volunteers known as Wikipedians through open collaboration using wiki software. Founded in 2001, it has become the world's largest and most-read reference work, providing access to the sum of human knowledge through its millions of articles. Hosted by the nonprofit Wikimedia Foundation, Wikipedia is funded primarily by reader donations and is a site that anyone with internet access can edit and improve. Key Characteristics Free Access: Wikipedia provides free access to information for everyone on the planet. Collaborative Creation: Articles are collaboratively written and maintained by a community of volunteers using wiki software. Anyone Can Edit: Most Wikipedia pages can be edited by anyone with an internet connection, allowing for continuous improvement and correction. Vast Scope: It is the largest reference work in history, with millions of articles in hundreds of languages. Nonprofit Hosting: The Wikimedia Foundation hosts Wikipedia and is funded by donations. How it Works Wiki Software: Wikipedia utilizes wiki software, which allows users to easily create and develop articles through open collaboration. Volunteer Community: The articles are written by volunteers called Wikipedians, who work together to add and improve content. Open Editing: Users can edit pages to add information, correct errors, improve readability, or add free-to-use images. Discussion Pages: Each article has a "Talk" page where users can suggest improvements and corrections. Origin and Growth Wikipedia originated from Nupedia, a more structured online encyclopedia started in 2000. On January 15, 2001, Wikipedia was launched as a supplementary feature of Nupedia, and it was quickly relaunched as an independent website. Since its founding, it has grown rapidly to become the world's largest reference website, attracting billions of views monthly.
